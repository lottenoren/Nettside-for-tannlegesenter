Project Requirements

By: Aurora, Eeman, Lana, Lotte, Klara and Helene
Date: 26. September 2023

About our client

Our client, Heidi Dalåmo, is a dentist from Trondheim, Norway. She was born and raised in Meråker, Norway, but did her studies in Stokholm, Sweden. She moved back to Norway in 2010 where she now works as a private dentist at Valentinlyst. She currently recieves appointment bookings via Valentinlystssenteret's website, which is something she would like to change as she works private. That is why we plan to make a website for her.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of this website is to attract attention to Heidi's dental office and to make her services accessible to a broader customer group. Heidi has previously booked her customers through the Valentinlyst dental office, but now she wants her own website, which will allow her clients to contact her office directly. Heidi values the aesthetics of her workspace, and has chosen to go with a pink theme in her dental office, which is something we will include in the website.

The goal of this project is to make Heidi's dental services more available, including having a functional and user friendly website that provides her services to the customers in a comprehensible way. In order to do this we want to include different sections, such as a booking section, an about us-section, a list of treatments with the price listed and so on, so that it's easy for the user to navigate through the website.

Audience

Our website's main audience will be those who want to book an appointment with Heidi, through the booking section of the site. Heidi only does adult treatments, so this audience will consist of people over the age of 21. Additionally, those who wish to learn about the different treatments offered, research prices or contact the dental office, will also make up a part of the audience.

The content of the website and how it is organized

We plan to have a front page with a menu and a site map. The site map will include links to the following pages:

  • About us: This section will give some information about the clinic and Heidi. We will also include some pictures of Heidi, the office, etc.
  • Treatments: In this section, we will present an overview of the treatments that the clinic offers.
  • Prices: We want to have a side that shows the prices on the different treatments the clinic offers, so the customers knows how much the treatment will cost.
  • Booking: In the menu, we want to include shortcuts to an appointment booking page. We have not decide how this page will be structured, but we want the customers to be able to send a message to the clinic to book a treatment.
  • Contact us: In this section, we want to present the contact information to the clinic. This includes a phone number, mail address, clinic address and different transport options to the clinic.

Functional requirements

  • Appointment Booking System: Users should be able to easily book appointments with the dentist through the website. The system should provide available time slots and allow users to select their preferred date and time.
  • Reference Page: The website should include a reference or information page where patients can find details about the dentist's qualifications, expertise, and any relevant certifications or awards.
  • Treatment Information: Include detailed information about common dental treatments and procedures offered by the dentist, along with any associated costs and recovery instructions.
  • Patient Reviews and Testimonials: Allow patients to leave reviews and testimonials to build trust and credibility.
  • Emergency Contact: Display emergency contact information prominently for after-hours or urgent dental needs.

Non-functional requirements

  • Fast Loading Times: The website should load quickly, with pages and images optimized for minimal loading times to enhance user experience.
  • Mobile Responsiveness: Ensure that the website is fully responsive and compatible with various devices, including smartphones and tablets.
  • Cross-Browser Compatibility: The website should function consistently and correctly across different web browsers (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  • User-Friendly Navigation: Make sure the website has an intuitive and user-friendly navigation structure to help visitors find information easily.
  • Integration with Mail Apps: Implement a feature that allows users to easily contact the dentist through their preferred mail app with the delivery address pre-filled for convenience.
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Optimize the website for search engines to improve its visibility and ranking in search results.

Final destination

When the project is finished, the client will decide if she wants to keep the website open for further use. If she decides to keep the site open, we will find a relevant domain name to keep it open on, i.e. tannlegeheididalaamo.no
